Record your screen and webcam

Welcome to my screen and audio recording web app! With this free and open-source tool, you can effortlessly capture your screen, webcam, and microphone. Whether you need to create tutorials, showcase presentations, or simply share your thoughts, this app has got you covered.

To begin, grant your browser permission to access your screen and/or webcam. Once you're ready, hit the record button, and my app will start capturing high-quality video and audio separately. This separation allows for greater flexibility when it comes to downloading and editing your recordings.

After finishing your recording, you'll have the option to preview both the video and audio tracks. If everything looks good, you can either download them individually or conveniently merge them into a single file. The choice is yours!

I've designed this app to be user-friendly and accessible. No need to worry about complicated setups or hidden costs. Simply enjoy the seamless experience of recording, previewing, and downloading your recordings with ease.

Join the community of content creators, educators, and professionals who rely on my app for their multimedia needs. Start exploring the possibilities today!
